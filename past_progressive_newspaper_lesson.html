Grammar Reference — Past Progressive

📖 Past Progressive (= Past Continuous)

We use this tense for an action that was in progress (ongoing) at a specific time in the past. It is often interrupted by a shorter Past Simple action.

🇮🇱 עבר ממושך — משמש לתיאור פעולה שהייתה בעיצומה בנקודת זמן מסוימת בעבר. לעתים קרובות פעולה קצרה יותר (בעבר פשוט) מפסיקה אותה.

⚡ Formation

was / were + verb-ing

I was walking home.
She was sitting at the table.
They were running in the park.

❌ He were working → WRONG
✅ He was working → CORRECT

בניה: was/were + פועל עם ing
I/He/She/It → was + ing
You/We/They → were + ing

🔗 With While / When

while + Past Progressive
While I was working, the monkey appeared.

when + Past Simple
When she arrived, they were eating.

⚡ The shorter action = Past Simple
⏳ The longer/ongoing action = Past Progressive

while = בזמן ש / כאשר (+ פעולה ממושכת)
when = כאשר (+ פעולה קצרה/פתאומית)
הפעולה הארוכה = עבר ממושך | הקצרה = עבר פשוט

📝 Negative Form

was not (wasn't) + verb-ing
were not (weren't) + verb-ing

I wasn't working — I was sleeping!
They weren't driving slowly.

שלילה: wasn't / weren't + verb-ing
לא הייתי... / לא היו...

❓ Questions

Was/Were + subject + verb-ing?

What were you doing at 7 o'clock?
Was she walking her dog?

שאלה: Was/Were מגיע לפני הנושא
מה עשית? / האם היא...?

Writing Preparation — Newspaper Article · הכנה לכתיבת כתבה עיתונאית
Newspaper articles answer the Wh- questions: Who? What? Where? When? Why? They use Past Simple and Past Progressive, and connectors to link ideas clearly. 📰 כתבה עיתונאית — כתבה עיתונאית עונה על שאלות: מי? מה? איפה? מתי? למה? היא כתובה בעבר פשוט ועבר ממושך, ומשתמשת במחברים כדי לקשר בין הרעיונות.

When writing a newspaper article, use connectors to give more information or show contrast:

📌 and — adds information  |  but / however — shows contrast
📌 in addition / also — adds more  |  even though — unexpected result

⚠️ We do not usually begin a sentence with and or but.

מחברים — connectors:
and = וגם (מוסיף מידע)  |  but = אבל (ניגוד)
however = אולם / עם זאת (ניגוד — בתחילת משפט)  |  also = גם
in addition = בנוסף לכך  |  even though = למרות ש
⚠️ and ו-but בדרך כלל לא מתחילים בהם משפט!
